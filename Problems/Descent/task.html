Descent

The selection sort algorithm we provide in this topic sorts the list in ascending order. Modify it so that it sorts the elements in descending order.

You do NOT need to process input or call the function, the example is for you to test your program.


Sample Input:
8 11 5 19 3 6 9

Sample Output:
19 11 9 8 6 5 3



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 15 seconds
