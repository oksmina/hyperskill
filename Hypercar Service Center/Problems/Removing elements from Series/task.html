Removing elements from Series

Imagine you have a Series olympics that stores the names of the cities of the Summer Olympics between 2000 and 2020. The index is set to the year of the games (as an integer), and the city names are represented as strings. Here is a fragment of the Series:

2000            Sydney
2004            Athens
...
2020             Tokyo
dtype: object

However, the games of 2020 were cancelled because of the COVID-19 outbreak.

Define a function that removes the corresponding row from the olympics Series and returns the resulting Series object.



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 15 seconds

Caution

You may see errors in your code or in execution results because of missing context. Don't mind those; simply write your solution and click Check.
